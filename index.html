Rudolph
vs
The Blobs
Press R To Start
<-

+ A/D or Left/Right arrow keys to turn.

+ W or Up arrow key to activate reindeer sparkle thrusters.

+ Space Bar or F to fire cookies.

<-

Drawn to Earth by the glow of string lights and the aroma of baked goods, The Blobs have come en masse to devour the planet, ruining various seasonal traditions in the process. Fortunately, the ambitious secular holiday hero Rudolph's blobby-sense is tingling, and he's off to space to blast them all to kingdom come with his cookie cannon! It's up to you to help him rid us of the hungry hordes!
